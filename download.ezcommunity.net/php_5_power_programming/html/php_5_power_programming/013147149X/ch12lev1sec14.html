12.14. Uploading

The final step is to actually submit the code to the pear.php.net site.

12.14.1. Upload Release

When you have tested your release thoroughly and it is ready for publication, go to http://pear.php.net/release-upload.php to upload it.

When the upload is complete, the PEAR web site runs some sanity checks on your package and displays a verification screen. Check the information presented carefully before clicking "Verify Release."

After you verify the release, it is published.

12.14.2. Finished!

Congratulations, you have just published a PEAR release! It will appear in the "Recent Releases" column on http://pear.php.net/ and an announcement email will go out to pear-announce@lists.php.net.
